## English

**Short description (≤80):**
A tiny pixel companion living in your real weather. Calm, cozy, always with you.

**Full description (≤4000):**
Weatherling is a tiny pixel creature that lives in your real weather, your real time of
day, and your real season. If it's raining outside, it's raining on them too. They sleep
through your night and wake with your morning. As alive as a game, as calm as an app.

🌦️ Real weather — Real conditions from your location (or a city you pick): rain, snow,
fog, storms on your screen.
🌙 Real time — Sunrise, sunset, night; a starry sky with the correct moon phase.
🌱 Your world — A companion that mirrors your age: playful when young, serene and wise when old.
🍎 Gentle care — Feed, play, rest, love. No punishment, no death — just a warm bond.
🕊️ Optional tradition — Your companion can keep a gentle spiritual rhythm if you want. Never forced.
🎨 Cozy pixel art — Soft light, calm lo-fi feel, little joys in every tap.

No ads. No pay-to-win. Just calm.
